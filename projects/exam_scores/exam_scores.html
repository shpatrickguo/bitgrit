Analyzing exam scores#

üìñ Background#

Client is an administrator at a large school. The school makes every student take year-end math, reading, and writing exams.

Let‚Äôs analyze the score results. The school‚Äôs principal wants to know if test preparation courses are helpful. She also wants to explore the effect of parental education level on test scores.

üíæ The data#

The file has the following fields (source):#

  • ‚Äúgender‚Äù - male / female

  • ‚Äúrace/ethnicity‚Äù - one of 5 combinations of race/ethnicity

  • ‚Äúparent_education_level‚Äù - highest education level of either parent

  • ‚Äúlunch‚Äù - whether the student receives free/reduced or standard lunch

  • ‚Äútest_prep_course‚Äù - whether the student took the test preparation course

  • ‚Äúmath‚Äù - exam score in math

  • ‚Äúreading‚Äù - exam score in reading

  • ‚Äúwriting‚Äù - exam score in writing
